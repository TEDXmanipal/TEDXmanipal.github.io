ABOUT US

TEDx was created in the spirit of TED's mission, "ideas worth spreading." It supports independent organizers who want to create a TED-like event in their own community. Aligned with this idea, TEDxManipalUniversity was born, and was a smashing success in its two editions, in 2017 and 2018. And now, we are back with a new look, TEDxManipal 2019. This year, we have an array of exemplary speakers who will be sharing their journey, parallel to the theme: Cognizance.





X

The purpose behind every TEDx event is to try and quench an insatiable thirst for knowledge and make people aware of the amazing things that go around in the world. Knowledge leads and moulds minds, inspires and encourages. In order to convey this very message, the chosen theme for TEDxManipal 2019 is ‘Cognizance’. With this edition, TEDxManipal aims on bringing inspiration to the 'Cognizance' of the masses.
